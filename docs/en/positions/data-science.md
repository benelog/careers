# Data & Analytics (Naver Search)

## Data Scientist

[Data & Analytics (DnA) team @ Naver Search](../teams/naver-search.md#data-science) is looking for passionate and talented data scientists at various levels (Junior / Senior / Manager). Data Scientists define new analytical problems and solutions, work with various partners in product and engineering to build solutions in new models and tools, and eventually deliver outcomes in reports and code to maximize technical and business impact. We also encourage the outcomes to be shared in major academic and industrial conferences.&#x20;

### Problem Areas

* User Modeling in Intelligent (Search, Recommendation and Conversational) Systems
* Research in Novel Metrics and Evaluation Techniques for Intelligent Systems
* Develop and Improve the Practice of Offline and Online (A/B) Experiments&#x20;
* Causal Inference with Applications to Large-scale Industry Data / Problems &#x20;

### Requirements

* Proof of quantitive and technical skills for all candidates&#x20;
* 3+ Years of experience in relevant areas for senior candidates
* Relevant areas include crowdsourcing, A/B evaluation, causal inference and model-based metric development&#x20;

## Data / Software Engineer

[Data & Analytics (DnA) team @ Naver Search](../teams/naver-search.md#data-science) is looking for passionate and talented data and software engineers at various levels (Junior / Senior / Manager). Data Engineers build large-scale data pipelines and infrastructure which will power various data-driven decision making and business intelligence. Software engineers build analytical ecosystem and online / offline evaluation platforms which will empower data scientists and analysts.&#x20;

### Problem Areas

* Large-scale / real-time data pipeline development and optimization
* Building state-of-the-art Offline (crowdsourcing) and Online (A/B) Evaluation Platforms
* Building and improving platforms and tools for data scientists and analysts&#x20;

### Requirements

* Proof of quantitive and technical skills for all candidates&#x20;
* 3+ Years of experience in relevant areas for senior candidates
* Relevant areas include Hadoop / Spark / Scala / Docker / Kubenetes / Front-end & Full-stack Web Development

### Mission

* Perform impactive long-term AI research to contribute to AI community
* Contribute to global AI R\&D leadership of NAVER CLOVA&#x20;
* Lead to sustainable growth of all the users and players (SME, partners, creators) on NAVER platform ecosystem as well as NAVER.
